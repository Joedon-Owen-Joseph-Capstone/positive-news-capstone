Persona

Name: Chris Everton

Age: 36

Occupation: Firefighter

Tech: Dell XPS 13 laptop with Windows 11, Samsung Galaxy s24 with Android OS 14, and the latest up-to-date version of Google Chrome.

Proficiency: Chris is very proficient with tech.

Love/Hate: Chris loves tech.

Attitudes & Behaviors: Chris is a tech-savvy individual who values efficiency and functionality in digital platforms. Chris is also passionate about staying informed but often feels overwhelmed by the constant barrage of negative news in mainstream media.

Frustrations & Needs: Overwhelmed by Negative News: Chris feels that mainstream media tends to focus more on negative stories, leading to increased stress and anxiety. Time Constraints: Due to the demanding nature of his job, Chris has limited time for leisure activities. He needs a News platform that efficiently delivers positive stories without requiring significant time investment. Lack of Personalization: Chris wishes to see news stories tailored to his interests and preferences, ensuring a more engaging and relevant experience.

Goals: Stay Informed Positively: Alex wants to keep up with current events but in a way that uplifts and inspires him rather than contributing to stress. Efficient News Consumption: Given his busy schedule, Alex aims to find a positive news aggregator that allows him to quickly catch up on uplifting stories during short breaks.

User Story: As a casual user I want to, quickly access positive news stories to uplift my mood.

Use Case: Chris wants to incorporate positivity into his daily routine amid the demands of his job. He uses PNN to stay informed about uplifting stories.

Pre-condition:

  • Chris has an active PNN account.
  • Chris has PNN open on his browser and is logged into his account

Post-condition:

  • Chris has been uplifted by the positive news he looked at.

Interaction Flow:

  • Chris opens PNN to a logged-in account.
  • PNN presents his home feed.
  • Chris browses articles until he finds one he wants to read and clicks on it.
  • PNN re-directs Chris to the articles page.
  • Chris reads the article and clicks back to PNN.
  • PNN re-directs Chris back to his home feed.
  • Chris clicks the filter button at the top of the page.
  • PNN opens a menu to filter tags.
  • Chris selects the “Health and Wellness” filter tag.
  • PNN re-directs Chris to a page of different articles relating to health and wellness.
  • Chris browses through articles and feels positively uplifted by the articles he has read.
