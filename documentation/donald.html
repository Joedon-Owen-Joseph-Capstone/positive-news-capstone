Persona

Name: Donald Bump

Age: 27

Occupation: Journalist

Tech: Macbook Pro M3 chip running macOS Sonoma 14.2.1, iPhone 12 pro running IOS 17.3, latest version of the Safari browser.

Proficiency: Donald has an intermediate proficiency with tech.

Love/Hate: Donald loves tech.

Attitudes & Behaviors: Donald is somewhat tech-savvy individual who values clean design and accessibility in digital platforms. Donald is also outspoken and likes to give his two cents and express himself outwardly.

Frustrations & Needs: Donald is fed up with the interactions on major news publications websites and the stories they provide. He wants to see peoples thoughts and opinions on the positive stories he engages with.

Goals: Give his opinions towards the news stories he interacts with via commenting and rating the stories.

User Story: As a power user I want to like and comment on news stories without much hassle.

Use Case: Donald has been scrolling PNN for a while and comes across a post he enjoys, he wants to leave a comment about what he liked and a corresponding rating to indicate his sentiment.

Pre-condition:

Donald is on PNN, on an article he wants to interact with.

Post-condition:

Donald has successfully commented and rated the post in question.

Interaction Flow:

  • Donald clicks the like button listed in the engagement bar
  • PNN changes the like button color to indicate he interacted with the post
  • Donald then clicks the comment button listed below the post in that same engagement bar
  • PNN pops-up the comment tab for that post with a keyboard for him to type in
  • Donald types his desired comment and submits it via the submit button in the comment tab.
  • PNN uploads his comment to that post.
  • Donald then exits the comment tab for the post
  • PNN closes the comment section to the main article
  • Donald has successfully engaged with his desired post
