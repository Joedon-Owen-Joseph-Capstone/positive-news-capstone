Personas

Web Application: PNN (Positive News Network)

Name: Bucky

Technology

Devices: Uses his laptop/desktop/phone, computers are Windows 11 and his phone is a Samsung S24. Uses Google Chrome for his browser.

Proficiency: Highly proficient with computers primarily uses them for message boards.

Love or Hate?: Loves technology, maybe on it too much.

Attitudes & needs

Why use it?: He moderates PNN to keep all user interaction positive and promote a good environment on the website.

What need does this persona have that your application aims to fill?: Allows him to easily navigate and resolve issues on the website with little to no hassle.

Why choose your site over others?: He appreciates the work and effort put into PNN and wants to keep the positive effect it has on other safe.

Bucky is an Admin user of PNN.

Use Case

Since the website allows people to post their own positive stories they find around the web, there are some neerdowells who enjoy trying to post bad stories to the website, while the filter blocks most of them, some super hackers can get them through the filter and posted. Bucky needs to stop these evildoers and delete their posts as they are reported and remove them off the website.

User Story:

As an admin user Bucky wants to moderate posts on the website.

Interaction Flow

Pre-condition: Bucky has the report user and post in question available from his admin console.

Post-condition: Bucky deletes the negative post and restores the harmony within PNN.

  • Bucky logs into his admin account
  • The website brings him to his profile page
  • Bucky goes to his reported posts tab in his admin account
  • Website displays all reported posts.
  • Bucky clicks on the most recent reported post.
  • Website takes him to an overview of the post in question.
  • Bucky determines it is against the rules and clicks the remove post button.
  • The website removes the post from the website.
  • Peace prevails.
